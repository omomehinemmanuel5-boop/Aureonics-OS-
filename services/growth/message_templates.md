# LEX AUREON Outbound Message Pattern Library

## Founder Persona Hook
- Opener: Reference product launch, funding, or growth post.
- Pain hypothesis: Trust and compliance blockers appear after pilot traction.
- Value: Governance telemetry + policy enforcement without slowing model iteration.
- CTA: "Want me to show you a 2-minute live demo?"

## CTO Persona Hook
- Opener: Mention architecture/security insight.
- Pain hypothesis: Production AI systems fail auditability and incident explainability checks.
- Value: Real-time intervention trace, deterministic guardrails, governance scorecard.
- CTA: "Can I send available slots this week?"
